A Timeline of Disneyland

This was how my project had originally began; I had wanted to focus on something light and fun due to how serious things had become, and Disneyland has always been a place that I associate with happiness and ease. Growing up in California, my family and I would travel to Anaheim once a year to visit Disneyland - we would lazily walk around the park, revisiting our favorite rides and attractions, eating churros and Dole whips. I had wanted to use those memories of Disneyland to make a timeline of the park, but it felt as if the more I worked on it, the more unmotivated I became to finish it...
